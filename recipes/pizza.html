Yeah we're gonna get som mF pizza up in here

Description:

A pizza is an italian dish made from a bun, some kind of filling or topping, and usually has a layer of cheese on top.

    Ingredients:

  • Bun
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Your toppings of choice
  1. Preheat oven to 225 degrees celsius
  2. Spread dough out on your pan / pizza stone
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on bun
  4. Add toppings of choice
  5. Distribute cheese over entire pizza
  6. Cook for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
