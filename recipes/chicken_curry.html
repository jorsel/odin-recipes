Pad Thai

Description:

Indian pot dish with chicken

    Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Stock
  • Spices
  • Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  1. Chop Onion, turn to paste and set aside
  2. Chop garlic, turn to paste and set aside. Grate ginger on it
  3. Heat wok pan with oil
  4. Add spices to pan and mix for 30 seconds
  5. Add onion paste and cook until golden
  6. Add garlic and ginger mix and cook for 2 mminutes
  7. Add tomatoes and reduce under lid for 10 minutes
  8. Add chicken to pan and cook for 5 minutes until the chicken is covered in sauce.
  9. Add chicken stock and let simmer for 10 minutes
  10. DEVOUR
