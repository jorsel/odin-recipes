Pad Thai

Description:

Thai street food

    Ingredients:

  • Rice Noodles
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Wok Vegetables
  • Eggs

  2. Whisk the eggs
  3. Make sauce
  4. Cook noodles
  5. Bring out your wokpan and heat it. Cook chicken until you get a nice crust on it. Set aside.
  6. Cook onions until soft. Add the rest of the vegetables and cook for 3 minutes. Set aside.
